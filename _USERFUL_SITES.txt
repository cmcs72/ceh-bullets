 ===[ TOOLS ]===
  https://github.com/ShawnDEvans/smbmap
	SMBMap allows users to enumerate samba share drives across an entire domain. 
	List share drives, drive permissions, share contents, upload/download functionality, 
	file name auto-download pattern matching, and even execute remote commands. 
	This tool was designed with pen testing in mind, and is intended to simplify searching 
	for potentially sensitive data across large networks.
